Talent Management:

The Talent Management Programme provides participants with the skills, knowledge, best practices, tools, and resources needed to design and implement a Talent Management Strategy that is aligned to the business strategy. Having an integrated talent management program that impacts business goals is key to competing in a global economy. Using a proprietary approach, the participants will learn how to create the strategy and implementation plan, as well as design each of the talent management components to meet your organization's needs.

NCBA will make the incumbents learn how to implement the best practices in Workforce Planning, Talent Acquisition and Branding, Creating an Employer Value Proposition, Hiring and Selecting Strategies, Behavioral Interviewing, Onboarding Processes, Career Development, Engagement Practices, Retention Strategies, Performance and Feedback Processes, Creating a Talent Pipeline and Career Development Strategies, Developing High Potentials, Creating a Talent Pool, Conducting Calibration Discussions, and Identifying Metrics to Measure Results.

Our Talent Management Approach

Our approach to talent management is built on years of experience helping organisations to achieve success through people. We are the strategic partners of the experts in engagement and behavioural change and we know that delivering results needs more than just systems and processes. We take a people-centered, holistic approach to talent management using our immersive, experiential methodologies to help our clients deliver business results by realising the value and potential of their talent.

Therefore, when building strong and effective talent management propositions we focus not only on identifying talent but releasing it through a strong culture of talent and leadership. In our programmes and solutions, we bring together the many strands of talent management, from partnering with clients to build their talent management strategic agenda to building customised competency frameworks and delivering impactful and effective innovative outcomes. Globally, Impact works at every level of the leadership pipeline, supporting our members to build and consolidate their innovative and research capital.

Training & Talent Management

We nurture the knowledge and skills of students, academics and professionals in Entrepreneurial thinking and acting Innovation, Marketing & communication. Talent management helps industries to train their future employees while they are at universities. Our T&T arm offers solution under following verticals.

Management Development Program

NCBA offer tailor-made programmes, to address specific Learning & Training mandates from organizations. Under this format, sessions are devised and administered in collaboration with the client organization, at a mutually convenient venue and date. Currently NCBA is working with a host of companies spanning across top-of-the-league corporate houses to Public Sector Enterprises to Not-for-profit organizations, creating cutting-edge training modules that give them clout in a constantly changing global marketplace.

Faculty Development Program

NCBA`s Faculty Development Program is a unique program that covers essential aspects of business management teaching. The program will introduce you to effective approaches to teaching and also help you identify your own strengths and areas for improvement.

The program prepares professors/ faculties/ researchers to make outstanding contributions to their institutions through the development of critical teaching and leadership skills. Led by NCBA`s talent & skill development task force, The FDP attracts participants from diverse countries to provide highly rewarding learning experience. The program concentrates on teaching methodologies in entrepreneurship, education, research and development of curricula. The overall aim of the program is to improve the general quality of entrepreneurship, management and technology education in India.

Personality Development Program

Our PDP programme enhances student motivation for learning in general and for "employability" in particular. The programme sets out to develop within the curriculum the following set of skills generic across all disciplines, which would help the student to respond to the changing needs of employers and to support their career planning activities into the future.

Through a series of group discussions, introspection, games and self analysis, you will discover your inner strengths and learn to project the best in you. You will get insights into how the world views you. You will become radiant, charming and creative. Discover a new YOU through NCBA PDP.
