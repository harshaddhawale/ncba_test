Membership benefits

Annual membership package

Want to stay up-to-date on the latest in UIIN and university-industry interaction? Your annual membership package provides you with exactly that, with a hard-copy version of the University Industry Innovation Magazine and the Good and Next Practice books (organisational member only).

Knowledge & resources

It is hard to stay on top of the latest research and good practices. You might find the time to stay informed on what is going on in region or country. However, we provide you with the latest research, good practice examples and the latest concepts through a searchable database. Learn about the latest from e.g. Europe, the United States, Latina America and Australia.
Visibility

Sometimes being visible to others is all it takes to establish new relationships and market your own and your organisation’s skills. Expand your outreach and increase your visibility through the UIIN members’ directory, the UIIN Magazine or UIIN Blog.
Events & Discounts

Workshop and conferences are an excellent way to stay up-to-date, gather new knowledge and network with peers. As a UIIN member you get discounts to the UIIN professional education workshops, the UIIN conference series and events we support. Become an educated university-business professional and establish connections around the globe.
