NCBA is to facilitate an open dialogue, exchange of ideas and collaboration among academia, industry and government on critical industry issues. NCBA offers the platform that universities, business and government can work together to conduct research and to solve challenges faced by the industry and society. NCBA also strives to educate future business leaders through undergraduate research, student exchange, workshops and tutorials, training sessions, internships and Co-op programs among the University Centers and Industry Partners. In addition, the following benefits to the Members also strengthen the reasons to join the NCBA:

  • An ecosystem for pre-competitive, high-impact research and open innovation
  • Exposure to compelling new technology and service business ideas
  • State-of-the-art facilities and testbeds for advanced collaborative research projects
  • Access to world-class faculty and students for effective knowledge and technology transfer
  • Facilitation of leverage funding and collaborative arrangements among universities, industry, and government
  • An NCBA Affiliate program focused on student access and engagement while exploring deeper research synergies

Membership benefits

Opportunity to influence the Future Direction of Research

The NCBA membership is an exceptional opportunity for business and academia to map and guide research in directions that maximize benefits to the companies and society at large. By identifying needs for information and research that are not being addressed elsewhere, industry partners can help define research projects useful to both their short-term and long-term needs, while avoiding the costly investment for in-house research infrastructure.

Credibility of University Collaboration

The NCBA’s platform gives the collaborative research a high level of credibility in the industry, increasing its impact and acceptance in practice. Industry members benefit from being associated with this research and may use this association to influence clients and regulators important to their business.
Association with diverse industries

NCBA membership includes technology, ICT, manufacturers, architectural and engineering firms, facility managers, governmental organizations, and academic institutions. The open exchange of information improves the ability to solve problems and to more effectively apply research in their decision making. The consortium also generates opportunities for industry and academia to develop business connections within.
Participation in NCBA membership events

NCBA's membership conferences and other regular events provide a framework for collaboration between industry, Government and Academia. We curate events and insights that will be of interest to our members, including professional networking opportunities in an exclusive environment and research highlights delivered to your inbox.



Staff training and recruiting opportunities

In a recent survey, our partners spoke highly of the technical knowledge they gained through NCBA network. Several member firms send a large number of staff to attend NCBA’s events to advance their organizational knowledge base.


Timely Admittance to Research Results

Membership in NCBA provides members with a window on current advances in science and technology and other disciplines that are relevant to their industry. This acceleration of information exchange reduces the time for new technology and innovation to be put into practice, and broadens the knowledge that partners can use to serve their customers and clients.


Cost-effectiveness

By pooling membership fees, a company's research investments will be increased 15-20 fold by leveraging common investments.
