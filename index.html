About NCBA

The NCBA is a vibrant network of academics, practitioners and business professionals passionate about advancing Academia – industry interaction and association, entrepreneurial universities and collaborative innovation. NCBA members includes Higher Education Institution managers (e.g. Chancellors, vice chancellors), intermediaries e.g. technology transfer offices, mobility offices), researchers in various fields, business professionals (e.g. innovation managers or management) and policy makers.
