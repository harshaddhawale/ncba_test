LEADERSHIP BOARD

Thought Leadership

The NCBA has a unique Leadership Board of senior business leaders and Academicians nominated due to their meaningful contribution towards building up Industry – Academia relationship and for making it a powerful networking body.
The Leadership Board establishes the priorities for Academia - Business collaboration. By bringing together expertise from every single part of India and a range of economic sectors the Leadership Board can identify the challenges at the interface between Academic Institutions and business and how to work together on them.
In this way the Leadership Board guides the work of the NCBA, with their recommendations setting its priority areas.
The Leadership Board is a unique and influential body from NCBA, the only organisation bringing together universities and business to influence government and solve the country's challenges. The Leadership Board also monitors the high quality research and analysis which is central to effective understanding of Academia – business collaboration.
